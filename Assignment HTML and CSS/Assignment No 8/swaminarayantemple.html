Swaminarayan Temple

Swaminarayan Mandir is one of the main attractions in India situated in many parts across the country. Swaminarayan Mandir in Pune was into a tourist visiting the place from February 2017. The temple was located on the hills of Ambegaon. This is one of the places to visit in Pune on the trip to Pune. Tourists can spend their time with the family here by exploring the peaceful surroundings and worshipping Swaminarayan. One can also enjoy the light and sound show conducted in the evening at the temple. The canteen is situated inside the temple for refreshments. The temple premises contain some of the fountains that surrounded the temple. Lots of tourists visit this place every day.
